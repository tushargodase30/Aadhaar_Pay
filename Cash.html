Bunch Note Acceptor

State of the art, User friendly and Secure are three reasons why the Gl-CDK is a leader in its own right. It is a high speed self-service machine that accepts up to 100 banknotes in a single feed operation because of its high transport speed and efficient scanning time. Designed specifically with the customer in mind, the Gl-CDK not only displays straight forward instructions on its touch-screen panel as well as emits clear lighting indicators to guide the customer through a seamless transaction; it is also compliant with the Disability Discrimination Act (DDA) of U.K and the American for Disability Act (ADA).

Single Note Acceptor

It is equipped with an automatic acceptor feature that enables the customer to feed banknotes in any orientation or alignment without compromising the quality used for identification or verification. At the end of each successful transaction, a receipt with complete transaction will be provided. The Gl-CDK for bulk banknote deposit is ideal for banks whose ultimate goal is to enhance the level of customer service and satisfaction. Ergonomically designed with minimal footprint, this self- service machine can be easily introduced in 24-hour banking facilities or highly frequented places to meet the needs of customers who deem banking flexibility and convenience.
