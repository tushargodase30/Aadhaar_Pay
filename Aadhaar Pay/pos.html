Cheque Deposit Kiosk

To increase financial institutions automation and provide more comfort to customers, financial institutions uses Cheque Deposit Machines. The machines accept the cheques and also provides additional facility of account name, account number verification for deposition to the correct account.

Cash Deposit Machine

Cash Deposit Machine accepts multi-currency and also provides additional facility of account name, account number verification for deposition to the correct account. We provide variety in Cash Deposit Machines like, Bunch Note Acceptor, Bulk Note Acceptor, Single Note Acceptor.

Passbook Printing Kiosk

To increase financial institutions automation and provide more comfort to customers, financial institutions uses Passbook Printing Kiosk. The machines accept live customer data from bank CBS and print it on Customers Passbook.

Information Kiosk can be used as Internet Banking Kiosk, Bill Payment Kiosk, Information Kiosk, etc purpose. Kiosk is with high speed performance with features like, Receipt Print, On-screen Keyboard, On-screen Numpad, etc.
