Kiosk ATM

Welcome to our state-of-the-art Kiosk ATM, where convenience meets innovation! Our Kiosk ATM is designed to cater to a myriad of financial needs, ensuring a seamless and efficient banking experience for our customers. Explore the diverse features that make our Kiosk ATM stand out:

Key Features:
  • 24/7 Access: Our kiosk ATM is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, so you can bank on your schedule.
  • Cash Withdrawals: Easily withdraw cash from your account without the need to visit a bank branch.
  • Balance Inquiries: Check your account balance quickly and easily.
  • Transfer Funds: Transfer funds between your accounts or to another account within our network.
  • Receipt Printing: Print a receipt for your transaction for your records.

AePS

Experience hassle-free banking with our Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS). Perform various financial transactions using your Aadhaar card, including cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and fund transfers, all at the touch of a button.

  • Ease of use.
  • Safe and secure payment system.
  • Interoperability across various banks.
  • Reduced Cash Handling
  • Biometric Authentication
  • Increased Accessibility

UPI (Unified Payments Interface):

Seamlessly link your bank accounts and make instant transactions through our Kiosk ATM using the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). Transfer funds, pay bills, and manage your finances effortlessly with the power of UPI.

Key Features:
  • UPI allows instant bank transfers using mobile phones.
  • It operates 24/7 and is highly secure.
  • Users can pay bills, make purchases, and transfer funds easily.
  • UPI simplifies transactions by eliminating the need for cash or card details.
  • It promotes financial inclusion and interoperability between banks.

Credit Card Application

Apply for a credit card on the spot! Access a range of credit card options, compare features, and submit your application, giving you control over your financial choices.

  • Application Submission: Submit a credit card application form along with the required documents (ID proof, address proof, income proof, etc.) at the kiosk.
  • Credit Check: The bank or financial institution performs a credit check to assess your creditworthiness.
  • Credit Card Approval: If your application is approved, you will receive a credit card offer with the terms and conditions.
  • Credit Card Activation: Once you receive your credit card, you will need to activate it as per the instructions provided.
  • Credit Card Usage: Your credit card is now ready for use. You can make purchases and payments as per your credit limit.

Loan Application

Need financial assistance? Our Kiosk ATM simplifies the loan application process. Apply for personal loans, home loans, or other financial products directly from the kiosk, making the journey to financial stability smoother.

  • Document Verification: The bank or financial institution verifies your documents and assesses your eligibility for the loan.
  • Loan Approval: If your application is approved, you will receive a loan offer with the terms and conditions.
  • Loan Disbursement: Upon acceptance of the offer, the loan amount is disbursed to your bank account.

Cash Deposit

Need to deposit cash? Our kiosk allows you to deposit money into your bank account quickly and securely, without visiting a bank branch.

  • Card Insertion: Insert your ATM card into the card slot on the ATM machine.
  • PIN Entry: Enter your PIN to authenticate your identity.
  • Transaction Selection: Select the cash deposit option from the ATM menu.
  • Cash Insertion: Insert the cash into the designated slot on the ATM machine.
  • Transaction Confirmation: Confirm the cash deposit amount and complete the transaction.
  • Receipt Printing: Optionally, you can choose to print a receipt for your cash deposit transaction.

Revolutionizing Cash Withdrawal: QR Code & UPI at ATMs

In a digital age where convenience and efficiency are paramount, the integration of QR Code and UPI (Unified Payments Interface) technology into ATM transactions marks a significant leap forward in banking services. This innovative approach not only streamlines the cash withdrawal process but also brings forth a myriad of benefits for both banks and customers.

  • 1.Contactless: Minimize physical contact.
  • 2.Secure: Advanced encryption reduces fraud risk.
  • 3.Fast: Streamlined procedures for quicker transactions.
  • 4.Accessible: 24/7 availability for managing finances.
  • 5.Cost-efficient: Reduces operational costs for banks.
  • 6.User-friendly: Intuitive interface for easy transactions.
  • 7.Integrated: Seamless transition between online and offline banking.
  • 8.Cashless: Promotes a cashless economy.
  • 9.Transparent: Real-time transaction updates.
  • 10.Eco-friendly: Reduces the need for physical currency.
