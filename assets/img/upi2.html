Frequently Asked Questions

  • Online Sellers FAQ

Which SIM card works with this audio payment confirmation device?

We deliver Boombox, your payment soundbox along with the Airtel 4G SIM card which will work easily with the device. Though SIM rent is applicable on your Boombox per month.

How can I change the language of my Boombox?

To change the language of your Boombox, download the Mswipe Merchant app and under Profile > Language preferences you get two options - English & Hindi. Choose your preferred language to get sound confirmation with Boombox.

How can I receive payments from my customer through the Mswipe QR code?

We offer daily transaction settlements in the UPI VPA.

What is the battery backup of the Boombox device?

When the device is charged for 3-4 hours, the battery can last up to 10 hours.

How to charge the Boombox device?

You can charge the device easily with your mobile charger or the USB type-C cable provided with the device.

Can I use a single Boombox device for multiple QR codes?

Yes, you can use the same Boombox device for multiple types of QR codes.

Which UPI apps are supported on this UPI payment soundbox?

Mswipe QR code linked to Boombox can accept QR codes from any UPI apps like Google Pay, Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay, WhatsApp Pay, or BHIM.

How can I track my previous transactions?

You can replay up to the last 10 transactions on Boombox. Just long-press the Volume '-' key and then the last transaction will be displayed on the screen. If you want to check the transaction before that then short press the volume '+' key. You can also check the transaction history on the Merchant app with detailed reports & SOA.

In which languages Boombox is available?

Boombox is available in just English & Hindi language currently. You can also change the language anytime as per your preference after purchasing the device.

What is the cost of Boombox?

You can get your very own payment soundbox, also referred to as the Boombox, by paying a one-time installation fee of Rs.300 + GST & after that you will be charged rent of Rs.125 + GST every month.

What is a UPI payment?

Unified Payments Interface, also referred to as UPI payments, is an instant real-time payment system developed by the National Payments Corporation of India. The interface facilitates inter-bank peer-to-peer and person-to-merchant transactions.

Do the UPI payments work with all banks and financial institutions?

Yes, the UPI payments are accepted on all the bank networks.

Is it safe to use UPI for all kinds of transactions

Yes, UPI is a secure platform for online payments.

What is the UPI payment sound box?

Boombox is Mswipe's UPI payment sound box which provides instant notifications when you receive payment via sound and display. It is a small portable speaker for your daily instant payment alerts which comes with SIM-based connectivity

You can replay up to the last 10 transactions on Boombox. Just long-press the Volume '-' key and then the last transaction will be displayed on the screen. If you want to check the transaction before that then short press the volume '+' key. You can also check the transaction history on the Merchant app with detailed reports & SOA.

How do I turn off my UPI payment sound box?

You can long press the power button to turn off Boombox, your UPI payment sound box. For more information, follow the instructions in the manual.

How do I connect my Boombox?

To connect your Boombox select 'Activate Boombox' and follow the steps mentioned to get started. If you are already using Mswipe's MQR then you buy Boombox from Mswipe and select 'Link QR to Boombox' and start using Boombox. In case of queries, please reach out to 8291575059.
